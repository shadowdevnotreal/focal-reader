# Focal — Investor & Partner Pitch

> **One-liner:** Focal is a science-backed speed reading PWA that turns any text into a structured training session — with clinical diagnostics, AI comprehension testing, and full accessibility built in.

---

## The Problem

**Reading is the bottleneck for human knowledge absorption — and it's getting worse.**

- The average adult reads at **238 WPM** with ~60% comprehension (Taylor, 1965)
- Knowledge workers read 4–6 hours per day — that's 2+ hours of recoverable time
- 1 in 5 people have a reading-based learning disability (dyslexia, ADHD, visual processing disorder)
- Existing tools (Reeder, Spritz, BeeLine) either abandoned the market or never solved comprehension

**The gap:** speed reading apps have been speed-only. None measure whether you actually understood what you read. None are built for people who struggle to read in the first place.

---

## The Solution

**Focal is the first reading tool that treats speed and comprehension as a system.**

It combines three things no single tool has combined before:

| Layer | What It Does |
|-------|-------------|
| **RSVP Engine** | Eliminates saccadic eye movement — the biggest time waster in reading |
| **Psychometric Dashboard** | 6 clinical metrics that tell you *how* you read, not just *how fast* |
| **AI Assessment** | Comprehension quizzes generated and graded in real-time |

The result: readers don't just go faster. They know *exactly* where they're losing comprehension, and the app adapts to fix it.

---

## Product Highlights

### What makes Focal different

**1. Science-first onboarding**
New users get a structured 14-day training ramp (200 → 250 → 300+ WPM) grounded in RSVP adaptation research. Most tools drop users in cold. Focal builds the habit from day one.

**2. Adaptive typography**
Reading Display Mode watches your WPM and automatically switches between:
- **Scan** (light Inter, open spacing) for fast skimming
- **Deep** (bold Georgia) for slow comprehension
- **Default** for everything in between

No other speed reading tool changes its typography based on cognitive load.

**3. Clinical-grade diagnostics**

```
Consistency    ████████░░  82%   — WPM coefficient of variation
Focus          ██████████  94%   — Avg sustained attention span
Regressions    ████████░░  3/1K  — Backward navigation rate
Stamina        ███████░░░  76%   — WPM retention across session
Comprehension  █████████░  88%   — Quiz score average
Effective WPM  ████████░░  ≈265  — Speed × comprehension composite
```

These metrics are derived from the Hasbrouck & Tindal fluency norms and DIBELS assessment framework — the same framework used by reading specialists and clinical psychologists.

**4. Accessibility without compromise**
Focal was built accessibility-first — for people with dyslexia, ADHD, low vision, and visual processing disorders. OpenDyslexic and Atkinson Hyperlegible fonts are one click away. Every feature works keyboard-only. Every element has ARIA labels.

**5. Zero infrastructure**
The entire application is a single HTML file. No backend. No accounts. No data collection. Works offline. Installable as a PWA. This is not a limitation — it's a distribution superpower.
